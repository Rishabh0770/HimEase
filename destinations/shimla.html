Discover Shimla

Gulmarg, an exquisite jewel of the Indian-administered union territory of Jammu and Kashmir, is a captivating mountain resort nestled in the lap of the majestic Himalayas. Its name, quite literally translating to “Meadow of Flowers,” is a testament to the lush, rolling meadows blanketed in a kaleidoscope of wildflowers during the warmer months.

A cornerstone of Gulmarg’s charm lies in its breathtaking natural beauty. It’s a place where verdant meadows, carpeted with a myriad of blossoms, merge seamlessly with towering snow-clad peaks. Dominating the skyline is Mount Apharwat, an icon for adventure seekers. The Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world, offers thrilling rides to lofty altitudes, providing mesmerizing panoramic views that defy description.

Gulmarg’s allure extends far beyond aesthetics. It’s a year-round haven for outdoor enthusiasts. In winter, the town transforms into a powdery playground for skiers and snowboarders, attracting aficionados from around the globe. In summer, it beckons trekkers and hikers with its intricate network of trails that wind through emerald valleys and lead to pristine lakes, like the shimmering Alpather Lake. The town is also a paradise for golf enthusiasts, featuring one of the world’s highest golf courses, nestled at an elevation of over 2,600 meters.

FAQ'S

What is the best time to visit Gulmarg? ▼
The best time to visit Gulmarg depends on your interests. Summer (May to September) is perfect for trekking and enjoying the lush green meadows. Winter (December to February) is ideal for snow sports like skiing and snowboarding.
What is the Gulmarg Gondola, and is it a must-do activity? ▼
The Gulmarg Gondola is one of the world’s highest cable cars, offering stunning views of the Himalayas. Yes, it’s a must-do!
Can I go skiing or snowboarding in Gulmarg if I'm a beginner? ▼
Absolutely! Gulmarg offers beginner slopes and professional instructors to help you learn skiing and snowboarding.
Is it safe to travel to Gulmarg? ▼
Yes, Gulmarg is safe for tourists. However, it’s always best to check local travel advisories before planning your trip.
What are the dining options in Gulmarg? ▼
Gulmarg has a range of dining options, from local Kashmiri cuisine to international dishes, mainly served at hotels and resorts.
