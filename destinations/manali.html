How to Reach Manali

Manali is well connected by road to Delhi, Chandigarh, and other North Indian cities. The nearest airport is Bhuntar Airport (about 50 km away), with regular flights from Delhi. Volvo and private buses also operate comfortable overnight journeys, making road travel a popular choice.
