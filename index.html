01

What We Do ?

At The MilesOnWheel, we bring the magic of Himachal Pradesh closer to you. From serene hill stations like Shimla and Manali to the adventure trails of Spiti and Kasol, we design journeys that blend comfort, culture, and thrill. Our team curates handpicked itineraries, personalized stays, and seamless travel experiences so you can simply relax and soak in the beauty of the Himalayas while we take care of every detail.

02

Where We Go ?

With The MilesOnWheel, every corner of Himachal becomes accessible. From the snow-clad valleys of Manali and Spiti to the colonial charm of Shimla, the spiritual vibes of Dharamshala and McLeodganj, and the hidden gems of Kinnaur and Kasol — we take you to places where nature, culture, and adventure beautifully blend. Whether it’s a peaceful retreat or an adrenaline-filled trek, our journeys cover it all.
