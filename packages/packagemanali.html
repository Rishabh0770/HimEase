DAY 2 - MANALI LOCAL SIGHTSEEING ▼
  • After breakfast, proceed for a local sightseeing tour.
  • Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Temple & Hot Springs.
  • Explore the Manu Temple and surrounding villages.
  • Evening free to relax at Van Vihar Park.

DAY 3 - EXCURSION TO SOLANG VALLEY ▼
  • After breakfast, head out for a full-day excursion to Solang Valley.
  • Enjoy adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, skiing (seasonal).
  • Capture stunning views of the snow-capped Himalayas.
  • Return to Manali for dinner and overnight stay.

  • After breakfast, depart for a scenic drive to Kullu Valley.

  • After breakfast, visit the serene Jogini Waterfalls with a short trek.
  • Explore Tibetan Monastery and buy local handicrafts.
  • Enjoy some free time for last-minute shopping at Mall Road.
  • In the evening, bid farewell to Manali and start your return journey.
