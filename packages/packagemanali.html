Manali Delights Tour Package | 7 Days 6 Nights

Places Covered

Shimla » Manali » Solang Valley » Rohtang Pass

Experience the charm of Himachal with our “Manali Delights” package, thoughtfully designed to let you soak in the breathtaking beauty and vibrant culture of this Himalayan gem. Over seven memorable days, you’ll explore the lively town of Manali, enjoy thrilling adventures at Solang Valley and Rohtang Pass, and unwind amidst the tranquil landscapes of Kullu. This package offers the perfect balance of relaxation, adventure, and cultural exploration, making it an unforgettable Himalayan getaway.

What To Expect !

In “Manali Delights” (7 Days, 6 Nights), expect an unforgettable journey through the breathtaking valleys of Himachal. Begin with a warm welcome in Manali, where you’ll stay in cozy accommodations amidst the Himalayas. Explore the town’s charm with visits to Hadimba Temple, Mall Road, and local markets. Embark on thrilling excursions to Solang Valley, known for adventure sports like paragliding and zorbing, and to Rohtang Pass, where you’ll witness snow-capped peaks and stunning landscapes. Continue to Kullu, famous for its lush valleys, temples, and vibrant culture. Throughout the trip, enjoy a harmonious mix of scenic beauty, adventure, and cultural exploration, making this Manali getaway truly memorable.

Tour Itinerary

This revised itinerary covers 6 nights and 7 days, offering the perfect blend of Manali’s natural beauty, adventure, and cultural charm. Travelers will get the chance to explore Manali town, enjoy thrilling activities at Solang Valley, witness the snow-clad peaks of Rohtang Pass, and soak in the serene landscapes of Kullu. The itinerary ensures a balanced mix of relaxation and adventure, making your Manali trip truly unforgettable.

Included
  • 6 Nights’ accommodation as per slab.
  • Breakfasts and Dinner in Hotel.
  • Transport services by Individual vehicle as per itinerary.
  • Assistance on arrival.
  • Check-In & Check-Out Time 12:00 Noon.
  • All green Taxes, driver bata, toll.
  • Tax/parking.
Excluded
  • Monument entry fees/camera fees.
  • Personal expenses (calls, laundry, etc.).
  • Drinks.
  • Lunch.
  • 5% GST.
  • Heater charges.
  • All Entrance Fees.
  • Anything not mentioned in “Cost Include”.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - ARRIVAL IN MANALI ▼
  • Arrive in Manali and receive a warm welcome.
  • Transfer to your hotel and complete check-in formalities.
  • Relax after the journey and enjoy the mountain vibes.
  • In the evening, take a stroll along Mall Road and explore local cafés.
  • Overnight stay in Manali.
DAY 2 - MANALI LOCAL SIGHTSEEING ▼
  • After breakfast, proceed for a local sightseeing tour.
  • Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Temple & Hot Springs.
  • Explore the Manu Temple and surrounding villages.
  • Evening free to relax at Van Vihar Park.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Manali.
DAY 3 - EXCURSION TO SOLANG VALLEY ▼
  • After breakfast, head out for a full-day excursion to Solang Valley.
  • Enjoy adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, skiing (seasonal).
  • Capture stunning views of the snow-capped Himalayas.
  • Return to Manali for dinner and overnight stay.
DAY 4 - EXCURSION TO ROHTANG PASS (if open) ▼
  • After breakfast, drive towards the famous Rohtang Pass (subject to permits & weather).
  • Enjoy breathtaking landscapes, glaciers, and snow adventure activities.
  • Photography session at high-altitude points.
  • Return to your hotel in the evening for dinner and overnight stay.
DAY 5 - KULLU VALLEY & NAGGAR CASTLE ▼
  • After breakfast, depart for a scenic drive to Kullu Valley.
  • Enjoy adventure sports like river rafting (optional).
  • Visit Naggar Castle and Roerich Art Gallery.
  • Return to Manali for overnight stay.
DAY 6 - MANIKARAN & KASOL EXCURSION ▼
  • After breakfast, proceed for an excursion to Kasol & Manikaran.
  • Explore the vibrant Kasol village (Mini Israel of India).
  • Visit Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara and enjoy the hot water springs.
  • Return to Manali in the evening for dinner and overnight stay.
DAY 7 - JOGINI WATERFALL & DEPARTURE ▼
  • After breakfast, visit the serene Jogini Waterfalls with a short trek.
  • Explore Tibetan Monastery and buy local handicrafts.
  • Enjoy some free time for last-minute shopping at Mall Road.
  • In the evening, bid farewell to Manali and start your return journey.
