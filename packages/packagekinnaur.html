Beyond Chitkul – The Last Village Trek | 7 Days 6 Nights

Places Covered

Shimla » Sangla Valley » Chitkul » Kalpa » Reckong Peo » Nako

Embark on an unforgettable journey with our “Beyond Chitkul – The Last Village Trek” package. This 7-day expedition takes you through the untouched beauty of Kinnaur, starting from Shimla and moving deep into the Himalayan heartland. Experience the apple orchards of Sangla, trek through the picturesque valley of Chitkul – the last Indian village near the Indo-Tibetan border, and soak in mesmerizing views of Kinnaur Kailash from Kalpa. Explore monasteries, pristine rivers, and high-altitude villages while enjoying the raw charm of the Himalayas. A blend of trekking, cultural immersion, and breathtaking landscapes – this journey is perfect for explorers and adventure lovers alike.

What To Expect !

In “Beyond Chitkul – The Last Village Trek” (7 Days, 6 Nights), expect a once-in-a-lifetime journey across the mystical valleys of Kinnaur. Starting from Shimla, travel through winding mountain roads to Sangla Valley, known for its apple orchards and the Baspa River. Trek to Chitkul, India’s last village, and experience its unique charm and scenic trails. Visit Kalpa, where majestic views of Kinnaur Kailash await, along with local monasteries and temples. Explore the high-altitude lake village of Nako, and witness the confluence of the Satluj and Spiti rivers at Khab. Expect a mix of offbeat trekking, serene landscapes, and the untouched culture of Himachal’s frontier villages.

Tour Itinerary

This carefully crafted 6 nights / 7 days itinerary covers the highlights of Kinnaur – offering a perfect balance of trekking, sightseeing, and cultural exploration. From the orchards of Sangla to the spiritual calm of Nako and the adventure of Chitkul, this package ensures a holistic Himalayan experience.

Included
  • 6 Nights’ accommodation in hotels/guesthouses.
  • Breakfasts and Dinners included.
  • Transport services by private vehicle as per itinerary.
  • Assistance on arrival.
  • Check-In & Check-Out at standard times.
  • Driver charges, tolls, and taxes included.
  • Parking and Green Taxes.
Excluded
  • Monument/Monastery entry fees.
  • Personal expenses (calls, laundry, shopping).
  • Drinks & snacks not mentioned.
  • Lunches.
  • 5% GST.
  • Heater charges (if applicable).
  • Any special permits.
  • Anything not mentioned in “Included”.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - ARRIVAL IN SHIMLA ▼
  • Arrive in Shimla and receive a warm welcome.
  • Transfer to your hotel and relax after the journey.
  • Evening stroll at Mall Road for local shopping and cafés.
  • Overnight stay in Shimla.
DAY 2 - DRIVE TO SANGLA VALLEY ▼
  • Post breakfast, scenic drive towards Sangla Valley (via Kufri & Rampur).
  • Check-in at the hotel/camp and relax amidst apple orchards.
  • Evening walk along the Baspa River.
  • Overnight stay in Sangla.
DAY 3 - TREK TO CHITKUL (THE LAST VILLAGE) ▼
  • After breakfast, start trek/drive to Chitkul, the last village near Indo-Tibetan border.
  • Explore traditional wooden houses and temples.
  • Enjoy riverside trails and breathtaking Himalayan views.
  • Overnight stay in Chitkul / Sangla.
DAY 4 - SANGLA TO KALPA ▼
  • Drive to Kalpa, famous for apple orchards and monasteries.
  • Visit Roghi (Suicide Point) for panoramic views.
  • Evening enjoy sunset views of Kinnaur Kailash.
  • Overnight stay in Kalpa.
DAY 5 - KALPA & RECKONG PEO SIGHTSEEING ▼
  • Visit Reckong Peo Monastery and local markets.
  • Explore ancient temples and Buddhist Gompas.
  • Relax in the serene village atmosphere.
  • Overnight stay in Kalpa.
DAY 6 - EXCURSION TO NAKO VILLAGE ▼
  • Drive towards Nako, a picturesque high-altitude village.
  • Visit Nako Lake and Monastery.
  • Stop at Khab – confluence of Satluj & Spiti Rivers.
  • Return to Kalpa for overnight stay.
DAY 7 - DEPARTURE ▼
  • After breakfast, check out and drive back to Shimla.
  • Enjoy scenic mountain views en route.
  • Tour ends with beautiful memories of Kinnaur & Chitkul.

Thrills of Sangla Valley | 5 Days 4 Nights

Places Covered

Shimla » Sangla Valley » Rakcham » Chitkul » Kalpa

Embark on an adventurous journey with our specially curated “Thrills of Sangla Valley” package. Over 5 action-packed days, discover the raw beauty of the Baspa River, hike through pine-filled valleys, and explore Chitkul, the last village on the Indo-Tibetan border. This trip offers a blend of offbeat adventure, cultural charm, and mesmerizing landscapes of Kinnaur. From apple orchards to snow-kissed peaks, this package is perfect for those who crave both thrill and tranquility.

What To Expect !

In “Thrills of Sangla Valley” (5 Days, 4 Nights), expect an authentic Himalayan adventure. Stay in cozy riverside camps or boutique homestays while waking up to views of snow-covered mountains. Enjoy scenic drives through apple orchards, visit ancient monasteries, and trek around Rakcham & Chitkul. Witness the breathtaking sunrise over Kinner Kailash from Kalpa. A mix of adventure, exploration, and peace—this package is a paradise for nature lovers and explorers.

Tour Itinerary

This 4 Nights / 5 Days itinerary is designed for adventurers who want to experience the untouched beauty of Kinnaur. From exploring Sangla Valley’s lush greenery to trekking through Chitkul, and from Rakcham’s hidden gems to Kalpa’s sacred landscapes, this package ensures a lifetime of memories.

  • Soft drinks/alcohol.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - ARRIVAL IN SHIMLA & TRANSFER TO SANGLA ▼
  • Arrive in Shimla and drive towards Sangla Valley.
  • Check-in to your riverside stay/camp.
  • Evening free to walk along the Baspa River.
  • Overnight stay in Sangla.
DAY 2 - SANGLA & RAKCHAM EXPLORATION ▼
  • After breakfast, explore Sangla Meadows with views of Kinnaur Kailash.
  • Visit Kamru Fort and local monasteries.
  • Proceed to Rakcham village, surrounded by pine forests.
  • Return to Sangla for overnight stay.
DAY 3 - CHITKUL – THE LAST VILLAGE ▼
  • Drive to Chitkul, the last inhabited village near the Indo-Tibetan border.
  • Enjoy short treks and riverside walks.
  • Taste the famous Hindustan ka Akhri Dhaba meal.
  • Return to Sangla for overnight stay.
DAY 4 - TRANSFER TO KALPA ▼
  • Check-out after breakfast and head towards Kalpa.
  • Visit Roghi Village & local monasteries.
  • Enjoy stunning sunset views of Kinner Kailash peaks.
  • Overnight stay in Kalpa.
DAY 5 - DEPARTURE ▼
  • Wake up early for a mesmerizing sunrise over Kinner Kailash.
  • Breakfast at hotel followed by check-out.
  • Drive back to Shimla with wonderful memories.
