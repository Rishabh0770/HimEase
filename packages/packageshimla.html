Classic Shimla Tour Package | 5 Days 4 Nights

Places Covered

Shimla » Kufri » Chail » Naldehra

Experience the timeless charm of Shimla with our “Classic Shimla” package. Over five relaxing days, enjoy the colonial elegance of the Mall Road, panoramic views from Jakhoo Temple, and the fun-filled adventures of Kufri. Visit serene hill spots like Naldehra and Chail for a peaceful retreat amidst pine forests and breathtaking landscapes. This package is the perfect blend of sightseeing, leisure, and cultural exploration in the Queen of Hills.

What To Expect !

On this “Classic Shimla” (5 Days, 4 Nights) journey, expect a refreshing escape to the scenic hill town. Stroll down the lively Mall Road, visit colonial landmarks, and admire panoramic mountain views. Enjoy a full-day excursion to Kufri for adventure and fun, while exploring apple orchards and pine forests. Continue your journey with serene visits to Chail and Naldehra, famous for their peaceful ambiance and natural beauty. This Shimla trip offers relaxation, family-friendly fun, and the serene charm of the Himalayas.

Tour Itinerary

This itinerary covers 4 nights and 5 days in Shimla, offering a mix of leisure, adventure, and cultural exploration. Discover Shimla’s colonial charm, indulge in Kufri’s adventure activities, and relax amidst the peaceful beauty of Chail and Naldehra.

Itinerary

DAY 1 - ARRIVAL IN SHIMLA ▼
  • Arrive in Shimla and transfer to your hotel.
  • Complete check-in and relax after the journey.
  • In the evening, take a leisurely walk along Mall Road & The Ridge.
  • Overnight stay in Shimla.
DAY 2 - SHIMLA LOCAL SIGHTSEEING ▼
  • After breakfast, visit Jakhoo Temple for panoramic views.
  • Explore Christ Church and colonial heritage buildings.
  • Stroll around Lakkar Bazaar for shopping.
  • Overnight stay in Shimla.
DAY 3 - EXCURSION TO KUFRI ▼
  • After breakfast, proceed to Kufri, famous for adventure and snow (seasonal).
  • Enjoy activities like horse riding & yak rides.
  • Visit Kufri Fun World and enjoy the scenic landscapes.
  • Return to Shimla for overnight stay.
DAY 4 - CHAIL & NALDEHRA EXCURSION ▼
  • After breakfast, head to Chail to explore the beautiful palace and cricket ground.
  • Continue to Naldehra, famous for its golf course and pine forests.
  • Enjoy peaceful walks amidst nature before returning to Shimla.
  • Overnight stay in Shimla.
DAY 5 - DEPARTURE FROM SHIMLA ▼
  • After breakfast, check out from your hotel.

  • Depart from Shimla with wonderful memories of your trip.
